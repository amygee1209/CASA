What is feminism?

(fem·i·nism) noun
The advocacy of womens rights on the basis of the equality of the sexes.

Why is feminism important in today's society

Despite beliefs that the war on women is over, it is safe to say we still have a long way to go in terms of gender equality. With the wage gap, rape culture, and lack of reproductive rights, the world we know has yet to be united. In order to further push for the much needed equality, knowledge surrounding feminism has to reach new ears and educate those who remain ignorant to the cause.

Who can be a feminist

Anyone! Feminists can range from all over the gender spectrum, as long as they share the ideals.
